Devlog 2 - 20/02/2025

Small update to the website, I have added back buttons to each of the dev pages. It was technically possible to just navigate back to the dev list page, using the navigation menu to the right --->
but a dedicated 'back' button is most intuitive here.


Another small change is the addition of different colored tags to distinguish the various dev diary entry types: General Weekly meetings are blue-purple, Lab meetings are blue-green, and these devlog updates are blue-yellow. Final colors may change, to be confirmed!


I have also added dedicated image support for thumbnails. This isn't being used now for any log, but it very likely will be used when we have concept art. This is just an exercise in future-proofing!
