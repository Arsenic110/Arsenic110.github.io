Date - 07/02/2025

During this meeting we settled on the majority of ideas we want to pursue with our game. We will include puzzles where tiles interact with eachother (such as push-buttons). We decided on a simple inventory system where the player picks up loot and can equip weapons they find. The game loop consists of exploring the map to find new rooms/locations, fighting the enemies within, picking up the loot and upgrading your player character with it.
