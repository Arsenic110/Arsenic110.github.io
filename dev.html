This is my dev diary for the game project.

I have been nominated as the producer, and as such, responsibility falls on me to keep the team on track.

Date - 31/01/2025

I have organized this meeting to establish the most basic information required to start work on the project. In particular, we decided to choose Godot as the game engine. We also made choices regarding usage of team organization tools like figma and trello. I made sure every member of the team had set up a GitHub account, as we will be using source control for this project.


The game will be a rouge-like top-down dungeon crawler, with underwater environments and with a cyberpunk setting. Exploration and randomness will play a role in the game loop.


As part of the agenda I prepared, we have nominated Peace as the Gameplay Designer and Caoimh as the Story Designer. I have also established the strenghts and weaknesses of each member and strategically positioned them in roles best suited for their skills.


I concluded our meeting by instructing Peace to create a GitHub repository and instantiate a new Godot project.

Date - 01/02/2025

I spent roughly 2 hours or so in creating this blog website to keep track of my team's progress. The website is relatively simple with a neutral design and colorful accents, as such there is not much to write about.
